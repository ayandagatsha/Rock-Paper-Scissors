Rock, Paper, Scissors

A simple implementation of grade-school classic “rock paper scissors” 
played on the devtools console. 

5 rounds are played and the winner is announced at the end. 

NB: While the first project of making a look-a-like webpage of the 
google homepage and using knowledge of how to move and place divs around
with HTML and CSS, this project is about using knowlege of JavaScript 
to add rules to what will happen when the user interacts with the created
program(set rules of instructions for computer to follow).
